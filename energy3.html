Types of energy

Animal energy is considered the first work energy used by man at the dawn of civilization when he used domestic animals in his work, then he proceeded and exploited the power of the wind to run his boats to distant horizons and exploited this energy with the growth of his civilization and used it as mechanical energy in running windmills and in running the wheels of grinding machines, saws of wood and water-raising pumps From wells and others, this is what is known as mechanical energy.

Energy type Energy type
mechanical the sum of the kinetic and potential energies of translational and rotational motion.
electrical Potential energy generated by or stored in electric fields .
magnetism Potential energy generated by or stored in magnetic fields .
gravitational potential energy Potential energy due to its position in a gravitational field or stored in a gravitational field.
chemical Potential energy due to chemical bonds.
ionize The potential energy that binds an electron to its atom or molecule.
nuclear The potential energy that binds nuclei together to form the nucleus of an atom (and nuclear reactions).
Chromodynamics The potential energy that binds quarks to form hadrons .
Flexible Potential energy due to deformation of a material (or its container) at which point a restoring force emerges.
mechanical wave Kinetic and potential energy in an elastic material due to a propagating deformation wave.
sound Kinetic and potential energy in a fluid due to sound propagating wave (a particular form of mechanical wave).
radioactive Potential energy stored in fields propagated by electromagnetic radiation , including light .
